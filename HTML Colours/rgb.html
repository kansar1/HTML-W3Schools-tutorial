RGB IN HTML

RGB

RGB is specified as rgb(red, green, blue)

Black is rgb(0,0,0)
White is rgb(255,255,255)

Shades of grey are created when r = g = b
e.g. rgb(200,200,200)

rgb(55, 175, 226)

RGBA

A is alpha value (controls transparency)

alpha is between 0.0 (fully transparent) to 1.0 (fully opaque)

rgba(55, 175, 226, 0.35)
