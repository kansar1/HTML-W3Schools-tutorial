HSL IN HTML

HSL

hsl(hue, saturation, lightness)

Hue is a colour wheel from 0.0 - 360.0
Saturation is a percentage of Saturation (0% - 100%)
Lightness is a percentage of light (0% - 100%)

hsl(0, 100%, 50%)

Saturation

Saturation is the intensity of a colour.
100% is pure colour, no gray.
50% is 50% gray and 50% colour.
100% is No colour at all.

hsl(0, 100%, 50%)
hsl(0, 75%, 50%)
hsl(0, 50%, 50%)
hsl(0, 25%, 50%)
hsl(0, 0%, 50%)

Lightness

Lightness is how bright the colour is.
0% is no light (black) and 100% is full light (white)

hsl(0, 100%, 100%)
hsl(0, 100%, 75%)
hsl(0, 100%, 50%)
hsl(0, 100%, 25%)
hsl(0, 100%, 0%)

Shades of gray

Set hue and saturation to 0 and then adjust lightness 0% to 100% to get lighter / darker shades

hsl(0, 0%, 25%)

HSLA

hsla(hue, saturation, lightness, alpha)

Alpha is a value between 0.0 (transparent) and 1.0 (opaque) determining transparency

hsla(0, 100%, 50%, 1.0)
hsla(0, 100%, 50%, 0.75)
hsla(0, 100%, 50%, 0.5)
hsla(0, 100%, 50%, 0.25)
hsla(0, 100%, 50%, 0.0)
